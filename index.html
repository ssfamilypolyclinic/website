About Us

In November 2023, SS Family Polyclinic became a part of Malaysia's healthcare industry as a paid healthcare provider. We founded by two very compassionate doctors with almost 13 years of expertise in medicine and working experience in government health clinics and government hospital.

Customers and patients can book slots for medical consultation in advance on our website for their convenience. We are also ready to do house calls with a very affordable rate. We are also trained to helps mother with newborns on their breast feeding journey. We are able to understand the struggles of working parents and ready to help them in their journey with a newborn.

Our clinic are currently operating from 8am to 11pm, seven days a week including public holidays and we hope to run a 24 hour clinic in the future to meet the ever-increasing demand of our patients.
